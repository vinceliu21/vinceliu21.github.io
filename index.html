About

I am a tech enthusiast and an adventurer. I am passionate about developing technologies that will transform the way humans live. I also love exploring the outdoors, hiking, roadtrips, and sports.

I've included some more information about myself below.

Philadelphia

I'm a South Jersey native, growing up on the opposite side of the Delaware River about 20 minutes outside of Philadelphia. I grew up a huge Philadelphia sports fan, but the two teams I'm most passionate about are the 76ers and Eagles.

Outdoors

Physical activity is one of my biggest must dos. Personally, it just feels great to get outside and move my body. I enjoy hiking, running, and many recreational sports.

Cooking

Cooking as a passion started out for me at a young age. I grew up eating my mother's home made Shanghainese food, while also watching cooking show every night before bed. It was amazing to see the dishes that chefs could make and stun their customers with.

Gaming

Videos games are another passion of mine. I have always enjoyed strategy based games and content with amazing graphics. Currently I own a PS4 where I spend time playing Destiny 2. I also appreciate a good round of DDR occoasionally!
